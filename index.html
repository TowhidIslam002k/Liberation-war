About the Archive

The Liberation War Archive is dedicated to preserving and sharing the history of Bangladesh's struggle for independence in 1971. Our mission is to collect, document, and provide access to historical materials related to the Liberation War, ensuring that future generations can learn from this pivotal moment in our nation's history.

The archive contains documents, photographs, audio recordings, and personal testimonies that tell the story of the war from multiple perspectives. We are committed to maintaining the integrity of these historical records while making them accessible to researchers, students, and the general public.
